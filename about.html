Who Am I?

I'm more interested in humanities and business than pure technology. My approach to tech is rooted in creating meaningful impact rather than just technical sophistication.

My journey has taken me from studying communications and mechatronics at ANU in Australia, to nano manufacturing at UPenn in the US, and now pursuing a PhD in NLP at the University of Melbourne. The pandemic led me to AI research somewhat by accident, but it's proven to be a fascinating path.

I've worked in various roles - coding, writing investment reports, product management - each teaching me something different about how technology intersects with human needs.
