التحيات الأساسية - Essential Greetings

Meeting Someone (اللقاء)

السلام عليكم
as-salāmu ʿalaykum
Peace be upon you (formal)
Universal Islamic greeting
أهلاً وسهلاً
ahlan wa sahlan
Welcome!
Welcoming guests
مرحباً
marḥaban
Hello! (informal)
Casual greeting

Asking How Someone Is (السؤال عن الحال)

كيف حالك؟
kayf ḥālak?
How are you? (to male)
Standard inquiry
كيف حالكِ؟
kayf ḥālik?
How are you? (to female)
Note the ِ ending

Common Responses (الردود الشائعة)

الحمد لله
al-ḥamdu lillāh
Praise be to God
Common response meaning "fine"
بخير، شكراً
bi-khayr, shukran
Fine, thank you
Simple positive response

Saying Goodbye (الوداع)

مع السلامة
maʿa as-salāma
Go with peace (goodbye)
Formal farewell
إلى اللقاء
ilā al-liqā'
Until we meet again
See you later
